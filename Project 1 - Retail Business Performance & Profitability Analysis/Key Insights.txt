KEY INSIGHTS

1. Top-Selling Product Type:
Canned Foods led the sales with over ₹1.06M, indicating high consumer
demand and effective pricing strategy in this category.

2. Most Profitable Region:
The West region showed the highest profit margin, with over 92%
profitability relative to sales — making it the most efficient region
operationally.

3. Sales Seasonality:
Sales peaked in November, suggesting a strong seasonal trend possibly linked
to holiday or festival buying behavior. Strategic campaigns during this month
can boost performance further.

4. Loss-Making Sub-Categories Identified:
Products such as Tuna, Jams, and Green Vegetables generated negative profit
margins and require evaluation for pricing, inventory, or promotional
changes.
